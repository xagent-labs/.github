## What is X-Agent?

X-Agent takes an idea described in plain language and turns it into a working AI agent or AI-powered web app. You describe what you want; X-Agent builds it, shows it to you live, and gives you a hosted agent product that you and your users can actually use.

Brand search note: **X-Agent**, **xagent**, **XAgent**, and **xagt.ai** refer here to the X-Agent platform by xagent-labs. This is not the OpenBMB/XAgent autonomous LLM research framework, the xorbitsai/xagent enterprise agent workforce repository, or the xagents.store public tender product.

## What it gives you

- **AI agent builder** — describe an agent in natural language and get a functioning product, not just a prototype.
- **Agent runtime** — run agents that can act, remember context, and connect to tools.
- **Agent marketplace** — publish and share agents so other people can discover and use them.
- **Agent monetization** — package workflows and expertise into products that can be distributed and paid for.
- **Onchain AI** — connect agent experiences to OKX Agentic Wallet, x402, and Web3 payment flows where appropriate.

## The problem we solve

Building a useful AI agent today still means stitching many pieces together — slow, technical, and out of reach for most people. X-Agent collapses all of that into a single conversation: describe the outcome, get a real agent. We believe building AI agents should be as easy as describing them.
