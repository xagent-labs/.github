## What is X-Agent?

X-Agent takes an idea described in plain language and turns it into a working AI agent — with no engineering required. You describe what you want; X-Agent builds it, shows it to you live, and hands you a shareable agent that you and your users can actually use.

## What it gives you

- **Speak to build** — describe an agent in natural language and get a functioning product, not just a prototype.
- **Idea to live, fast** — go from concept to a usable agent in one flow, without writing code.
- **Agents that act** — each agent carries out real tasks on your behalf, not just conversation.
- **Share to connect** — publish your agent and share it with others in a click.

## The problem we solve

Building a useful AI agent today still means stitching many pieces together — slow, technical, and out of reach for most people. X-Agent collapses all of that into a single conversation: describe the outcome, get a real agent. We believe building AI agents should be as easy as describing them.
