## What is X-Agent?

X-Agent takes an idea described in plain language and turns it into a working AI agent or AI-powered web app. You describe what you want; X-Agent builds it, shows it to you live, and gives you a hosted agent product that you and your users can actually use.

## What it gives you

- **Agent builder** — describe an agent in natural language and get a functioning product, not just a prototype.
- **Runtime** — run agents that can act, remember context, and connect to tools.
- **Marketplace** — publish and share agents so other people can discover and use them.
- **Monetization** — package workflows and expertise into products that can be distributed and paid for.
